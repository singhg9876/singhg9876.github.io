ABOUT US

Pro Health is a unique, proactive health management service committed to making the children healthier tomorrow than today. Pro Health strives to continuously improve the health and well-being of the school going children by organizing health camps to make a healthy Nation. We are committed to investing in school going children’s health and parents can count on us to help prevent and treat illness and injuries.



OUR BENIFITS

All of you must have heard the quote "Prevention is better than cure". Preventive checkups enable identification of health problems even before they occur.

  • Health Assessment: It allows parents to assess their child health and know the probability of getting sick.
  • Timely Precautions: It allows the child to take precautions to prevent the development of any disease or medical condition.
  • Early Diagnosis of Diseases: It enables doctors to diagnose a disease at an early stage where treatment is easily possible with no complications
  • Stay Healthy: Diet and exercises advised by the Nutrionists will keep your child healthy and fit.
  • Although the child seems to be fit within, a preventive health checkup is an important practice that one must inculcate in his/her daily life. Many who have availed preventive health tests and presumed that they are healthy were found with different diseases dwelling inside them.
