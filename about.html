ABOUT US

Pro Health is a unique, proactive health management service committed to making the children healthier tomorrow than today. Pro Health strives to continuously improve the health and well-being of the school going children by organizing health camps to make a healthy Nation. We are committed to investing in school going children’s health and parents can count on us to help prevent and treat illness and injuries.

Specifically, Our Health camps seek to provide both preventive and curative treatment for common conditions in the children of age group 5 to 15, and refer those who require specialized treatment to the relevant hospitals. We strive round the clock for the maintenance and improvement of the health of the students
