  • What are causes of Infertility?

    Male 30%, Female 40% and combined male & female causes 40 %.

  • What is the Success rate of IUI?

    10 % to 20 %, depending on female age factor and duration of marital life.

  • What is the Success Rate of IVF?

    30% to 60%, depending on female age and ovarian reserve and Male semen sample.

  • When IUI is indicated?

    Male factors – with low sperm count , some cases like

    • Sexual Dysfunction
    • Un explained Cause

    Female factors – Endometriosis

  • When IVF is indicated?

    Male Factors like Very low count, less than 5 million per ml
    Female factors – Repeated IUI failures and some cases like,

    • Severe Endometriosis
    • Blocked Fallopian Tubes
    • Un explained infertility
